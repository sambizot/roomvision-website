Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 13, 2025

RoomVision ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RoomVision mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address (when you sign in with Google or Apple)
  • Name (if provided by your sign-in provider)

User Content:

  • Photos of rooms you upload for design generation
  • AI-generated design images
  • Design preferences and prompts you enter

Usage Data:

  • Number of designs generated
  • Subscription status

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Generate AI-powered room designs
  • Save your design history
  • Process subscription payments
  • Improve our services

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (authentication)
  • Google Gemini AI (design generation)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Supabase (database)
  • Microsoft Azure (image storage)

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

Your design history is stored until you delete your account. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
